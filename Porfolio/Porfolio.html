Creativity is a very dense word. It does not only equate to the ability to create something, you should ask yourself if what you are creating is meaningful, useful and purposeful. If yes, then you are creative. If what you meant by “not being creative” as someone who is not very keen at aesthetic design, then yes you can still be good at web development. I know very good web developers who are not too keen with visual design, but are very good at formulating the best possible solutions to a problem. Most of them are backend developers. If you will be on a large team, your team may already have designers who will do the mockups for you that you can convert to a working prototype.
